Sprint Clock - Remote Countdown

Create a Remote Countdown

A Remote countdown continues the countdown even when you have left the page. When You click "start" the link to the countdown is copied in your clipboard. Use the link to access the countdown from anywhere, share the link to people who you want to join you on your amazing countdown. The countdown is relative to the viewer's timezone, this allows viewers from different timezones to share the same countdown relative to their time.
